About VK Electronics

Providing trusted, prompt, and certified home appliance repair and installation services across North Bengaluru.

Who We Are

At VK Electronics, we understand how essential home appliances are to your daily life. A broken refrigerator or a malfunctioning washing machine can disrupt your schedule and cause stress. That is why we are dedicated to providing same-day, doorstep repair and installation services that are fast, transparent, and highly reliable.

Based in Kogilu, Bengaluru, we have been serving local homeowners, tenants, and businesses in Yelahanka, Hebbal, Sahakar Nagar, and other North Bengaluru neighborhoods. Our team consists of background-verified, certified technicians with over 15 years of collective field experience troubleshooting leading global brands like LG, Samsung, Whirlpool, IFB, Bosch, and Godrej.

Our Core Values

  • Transparency: We inspect your appliance first and give you a detailed breakdown of the required spare parts and repair costs before starting any work. No hidden fees.
  • Quality Spares: We use only 100% genuine and manufacturer-approved components to ensure that your appliance operates smoothly and lasts for years to come.
  • Quick Turnaround: Our response time is one of the fastest in the region. We aim to reach your doorstep within 2 hours of scheduling your request.
  • Customer Trust: Every repair service is backed by a 90-day service warranty, giving you complete peace of mind.
